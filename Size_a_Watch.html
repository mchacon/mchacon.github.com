Determining Size

1.  Measure the wrist, allowing extra room if you would like the band to be looser. 


2.  Subtract the size of the watch face.

For a Gift

If you don’t know the size of the gift recipient, below are some average sizes and measurements:


extra-small girl = 2-3 years old

small girl = 4-5 years old

medium girl = 6-7 years old

large girl = 8-9 years old

extra-large girl = 10-13 years old


small woman = 4.5-5” band

medium woman = 5.5-6” band

large woman = 6-6.5” band

extra-large woman = 6.5-7” band
